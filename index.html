False Perspective brings you into their world where authenticity and honesty come first. Expect an intimate performance where multi-layered lyrics and ambiguous chords make you discover something new every time.

In 2026, they join forces with Hugo (clarinet, saxophone) and Eline (violin, vocals) to form an acoustic ensemble. With their unique blend of instruments, they create a captivating sound with wide-ranging dynamics.
